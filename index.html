Epic Store Free Game Checker

Welcome to our application!

This application checks if in your Steam account, you already own the current free game on the Epic Games store.

Instructions

  1. Go to the Steam store
  2. In the top right of the screen, click on your profile.
  3. Your profile settings must be set to public for this to work
  4. On your profile page, copy the userID in the url. It's not always a number but in general the url follows this format.
  5. Input the steamID in the form at the bottom of the page
  6. Click submit!

Alert

Error
